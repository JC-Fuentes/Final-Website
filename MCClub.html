MC Club
Our Motorcycle Club is responisble for our various operations in the desert. They are responsible for selling passports to citizens leaving the country as well as Identification cards. They are also crafty in growing organic compunds and are able to sell them to private institutions or other organizations. They also sell smaller amounts to smaller entities. From time to time they will go out and do supply runs for their small businesses as an organization for maximum efficiency and profit. They are also always looking for more drivers and workers. Drivers must have a valid Class C Drivers License as well as willingness to drive across the state for hours at a time. Workers must also be in a perfect state of health and either be a citizen of the United States of America or have a valid work visa.
